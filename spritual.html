Pilgrim's Progress

India is its billion people. And it is safe to say that these people define what spirituality in India is. The most prominent religions in the country are Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, Buddhism, Sikhism and Jainism. Apart from these there are several other schools of thoughts. The country is filled with many spiritual destinations which should not be missed by anyone looking for the true Indian experience. These are open and welcome to everyone.

Nestled in Pamban Island that is shaped like a conch, Ramanathaswamy Temple, in Tamil Nadu, is a quaint town that is connected to the Indian mainland by the 2-km-long Pamban bridge.
Kashi Vishvanath Temple is one of the most famous Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India. The temple stands on the western bank of the holy river Ganga.
Kedarnath is a town and Nagar Panchayat in Rudraprayag district of Uttarakhand, India, known primarily for the Kedarnath Temple. Kedarnath is the most remote of the four Chota Char Dham pilgrimage sites.
